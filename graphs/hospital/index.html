How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, October 19, 2021, there were 472 people in hospital, 74 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 17,843 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,932 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,306 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
