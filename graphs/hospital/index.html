Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, February 11, 2021, there were 984 people in hospital, 170 people in ICU, and 52 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,749 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,188 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,846 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
