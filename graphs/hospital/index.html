How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, September 16, 2021, there were 290 people in hospital, 65 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,163 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,800 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,179 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
