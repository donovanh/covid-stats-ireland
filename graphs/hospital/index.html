Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, February 22, 2021, there were 726 people in hospital, 156 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,513 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,253 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,137 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
