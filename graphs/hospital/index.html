Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, June 29, 2022, there were 776 people in hospital, 31 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 41,465 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,677 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,499 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
