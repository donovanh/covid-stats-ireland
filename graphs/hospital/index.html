How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, September 12, 2023, there were 284 people in hospital, 12 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 65,366 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,916 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,209 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
