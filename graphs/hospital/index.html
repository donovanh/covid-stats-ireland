How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, May 12, 2022, there were 242 people in hospital, 28 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 38,489 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,652 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,176 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
