How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, April 22, 2022, there were 599 people in hospital, 39 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 36,177 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,627 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,996 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
