Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 20, 2021, there were 179 people in hospital, 47 people in ICU, and 11 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,006 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,483 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,847 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
