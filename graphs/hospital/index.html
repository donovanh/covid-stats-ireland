Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 4, 2023, there were 387 people in hospital, 13 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 59,363 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,864 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,782 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
