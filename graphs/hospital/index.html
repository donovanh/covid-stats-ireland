How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, February 16, 2022, there were 631 people in hospital, 62 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 27,769 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,485 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,399 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
