Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, February 10, 2021, there were 1,032 people in hospital, 170 people in ICU, and 54 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,674 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,181 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,794 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
