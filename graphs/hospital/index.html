Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, August 29, 2023, there were 340 people in hospital, 19 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 64,595 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,913 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,180 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
