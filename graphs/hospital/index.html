Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, February 8, 2021, there were 1,212 people in hospital, 175 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,533 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,157 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,687 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
