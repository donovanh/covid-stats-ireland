Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, March 28, 2022, there were 1,624 people in hospital, 54 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 32,834 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,556 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,721 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
