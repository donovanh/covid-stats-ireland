Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, March 24, 2021, there were 329 people in hospital, 75 people in ICU, and 18 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,371 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,413 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,628 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
