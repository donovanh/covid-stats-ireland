Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, December 20, 2022, there were 624 people in hospital, 17 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 53,276 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,780 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,293 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
