Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 25, 2023, there were 272 people in hospital, 16 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 60,501 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,873 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,849 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
