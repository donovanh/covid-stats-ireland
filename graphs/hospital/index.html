Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, March 28, 2021, there were 322 people in hospital, 66 people in ICU, and 13 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,486 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,411 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,666 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
