Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 27, 2021, there were 153 people in hospital, 46 people in ICU, and 10 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,157 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,497 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,884 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
