Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, April 29, 2022, there were 379 people in hospital, 39 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 37,117 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,638 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,087 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
