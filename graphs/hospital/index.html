Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, November 17, 2021, there were 634 people in hospital, 114 people in ICU, and 43 deaths were reported.

A total of 19,744 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,089 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,609 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
