Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, August 15, 2023, there were 435 people in hospital, 10 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 63,752 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,903 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,148 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
