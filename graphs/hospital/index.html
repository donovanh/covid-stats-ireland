How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, March 18, 2021, there were 345 people in hospital, 82 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,233 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,366 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,566 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
