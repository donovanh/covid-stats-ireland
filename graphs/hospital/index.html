Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, January 31, 2023, there were 190 people in hospital, 27 people in ICU, and 8 deaths were reported.

A total of 56,502 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,837 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,515 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
