Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, March 10, 2022, there were 877 people in hospital, 41 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 30,183 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,519 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,590 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
