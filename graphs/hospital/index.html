Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, September 19, 2023, there were 278 people in hospital, 11 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 65,667 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,915 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,234 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
