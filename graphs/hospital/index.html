Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, September 22, 2021, there were 272 people in hospital, 63 people in ICU, and 30 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,295 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,826 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,209 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
