Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, March 26, 2021, there were 317 people in hospital, 66 people in ICU, and 20 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,423 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,409 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,651 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
