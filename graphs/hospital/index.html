How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, November 15, 2021, there were 622 people in hospital, 117 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 19,634 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,080 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,566 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
