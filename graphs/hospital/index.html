Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, December 12, 2021, there were 504 people in hospital, 109 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 20,979 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,224 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,788 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
