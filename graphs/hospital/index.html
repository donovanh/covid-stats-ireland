Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, September 14, 2022, there were 233 people in hospital, 13 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 47,830 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,733 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,901 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
