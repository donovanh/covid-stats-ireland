How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, February 22, 2022, there were 608 people in hospital, 54 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 28,475 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,494 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,452 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
