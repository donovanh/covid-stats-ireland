Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, February 26, 2021, there were 574 people in hospital, 136 people in ICU, and 29 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,789 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,291 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,300 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
