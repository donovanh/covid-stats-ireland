How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, September 20, 2021, there were 297 people in hospital, 63 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,230 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,818 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,179 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
