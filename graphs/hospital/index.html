How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, November 22, 2021, there were 685 people in hospital, 126 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 20,024 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,110 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,609 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
