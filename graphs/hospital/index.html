Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, March 13, 2021, there were 340 people in hospital, 85 people in ICU, and 16 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,070 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,358 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,534 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
