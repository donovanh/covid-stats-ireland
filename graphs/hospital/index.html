Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, November 15, 2022, there were 317 people in hospital, 16 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 50,902 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,760 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,141 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
