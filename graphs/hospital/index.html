Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, March 27, 2021, there were 304 people in hospital, 65 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,460 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,411 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,653 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
