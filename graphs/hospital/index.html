Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, July 18, 2023, there were 138 people in hospital, 7 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 62,552 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,900 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,097 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
