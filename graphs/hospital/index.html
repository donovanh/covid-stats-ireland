Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 22, 2022, there were 1,339 people in hospital, 61 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 31,754 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,544 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,680 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
