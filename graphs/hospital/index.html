Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, March 5, 2021, there were 426 people in hospital, 102 people in ICU, and 9 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,068 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,317 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,405 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
