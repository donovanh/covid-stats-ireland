Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, October 18, 2022, there were 451 people in hospital, 16 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 49,566 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,740 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,010 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
