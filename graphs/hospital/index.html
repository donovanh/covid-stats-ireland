Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, October 24, 2021, there were 471 people in hospital, 97 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 18,294 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,962 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,369 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
