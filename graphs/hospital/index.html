Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, January 31, 2021, there were 1,515 people in hospital, 211 people in ICU, and 15 deaths were reported.

A total of 10,873 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,059 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,307 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
