How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, May 10, 2021, there were 124 people in hospital, 31 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,387 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,540 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,921 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
