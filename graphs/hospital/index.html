Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, January 10, 2023, there were 573 people in hospital, 35 people in ICU, and 7 deaths were reported.

A total of 55,488 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,817 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,388 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
