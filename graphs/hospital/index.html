Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, April 29, 2021, there were 154 people in hospital, 44 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,205 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,505 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,899 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
