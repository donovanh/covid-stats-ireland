How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, September 27, 2021, there were 310 people in hospital, 66 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,432 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,837 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,209 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
