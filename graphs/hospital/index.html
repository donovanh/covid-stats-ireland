Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, February 24, 2021, there were 652 people in hospital, 136 people in ICU, and 56 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,647 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,271 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,237 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
