Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, February 21, 2021, there were 744 people in hospital, 151 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,381 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,252 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,136 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
