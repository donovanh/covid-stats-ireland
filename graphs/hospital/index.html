Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, December 4, 2021, there were 487 people in hospital, 110 people in ICU, and 8 deaths were reported.

A total of 20,621 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,197 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,707 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
