Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 21, 2023, there were 295 people in hospital, 7 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 58,593 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,861 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,750 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
