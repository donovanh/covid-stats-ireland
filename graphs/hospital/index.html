How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, November 4, 2021, there were 458 people in hospital, 90 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 18,954 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,010 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,492 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
