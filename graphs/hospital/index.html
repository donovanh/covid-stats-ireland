Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, December 15, 2021, there were 470 people in hospital, 105 people in ICU, and 7 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,176 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,251 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,835 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
