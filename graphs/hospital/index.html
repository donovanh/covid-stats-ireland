Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, March 20, 2021, there were 328 people in hospital, 87 people in ICU, and 9 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,219 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,398 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,585 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
