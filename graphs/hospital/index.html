How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, October 18, 2021, there were 484 people in hospital, 73 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 17,668 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,920 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,306 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
