Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, May 13, 2022, there were 235 people in hospital, 28 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 38,530 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,656 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,203 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
