How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, August 21, 2021, there were 259 people in hospital, 54 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,430 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,543 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,074 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
