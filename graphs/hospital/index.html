How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, September 26, 2021, there were 296 people in hospital, 65 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,366 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,831 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,209 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
