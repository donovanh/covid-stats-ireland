Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, December 16, 2021, there were 443 people in hospital, 105 people in ICU, and 7 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,235 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,255 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,835 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
