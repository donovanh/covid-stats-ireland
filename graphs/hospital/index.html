Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, February 23, 2021, there were 693 people in hospital, 151 people in ICU, and 45 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,575 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,260 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,181 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
