Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, August 31, 2022, there were 262 people in hospital, 15 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 47,157 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,724 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,839 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
