Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, September 5, 2023, there were 301 people in hospital, 12 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 64,955 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,915 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,200 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
