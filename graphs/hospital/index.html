Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 13, 2021, there were 206 people in hospital, 48 people in ICU, and 18 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,867 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,462 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,803 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
