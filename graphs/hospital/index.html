How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, June 13, 2023, there were 150 people in hospital, 11 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 61,998 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,891 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,028 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
