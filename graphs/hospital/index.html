Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, April 11, 2022, there were 1,081 people in hospital, 55 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 35,083 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,601 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,884 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
