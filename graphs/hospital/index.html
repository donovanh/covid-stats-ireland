Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, April 14, 2022, there were 904 people in hospital, 57 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 35,470 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,611 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,932 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
