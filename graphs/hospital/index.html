Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, September 15, 2021, there were 292 people in hospital, 65 people in ICU, and 24 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,133 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,791 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,179 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
