How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, March 30, 2022, there were 1,610 people in hospital, 49 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 33,405 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,560 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,740 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
