Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 15, 2022, there were 1,047 people in hospital, 42 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 30,870 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,534 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,636 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
