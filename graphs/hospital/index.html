Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, May 23, 2023, there were 253 people in hospital, 8 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 61,317 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,882 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,960 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
