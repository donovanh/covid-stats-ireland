How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, October 12, 2021, there were 402 people in hospital, 73 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 17,017 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,886 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,280 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
