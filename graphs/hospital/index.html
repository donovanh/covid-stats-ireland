Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, May 11, 2021, there were 117 people in hospital, 34 people in ICU, and 9 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,396 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,541 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,929 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
