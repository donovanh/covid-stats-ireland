Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, April 13, 2022, there were 933 people in hospital, 58 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 35,376 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,609 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,919 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
