Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, January 15, 2022, there were 940 people in hospital, 89 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,662 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,300 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,035 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
