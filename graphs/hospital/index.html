Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, April 20, 2022, there were 706 people in hospital, 42 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 35,960 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,623 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,981 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
