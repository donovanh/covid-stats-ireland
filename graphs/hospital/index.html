Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 12, 2022, there were 1,004 people in hospital, 53 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 35,204 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,605 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,903 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
