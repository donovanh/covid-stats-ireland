Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, March 10, 2021, there were 370 people in hospital, 92 people in ICU, and 47 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,161 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,340 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,499 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
