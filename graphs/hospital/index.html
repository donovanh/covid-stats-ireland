Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 2, 2021, there were 498 people in hospital, 117 people in ICU, and 14 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,984 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,303 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,333 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
