Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, June 20, 2023, there were 112 people in hospital, 9 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 62,135 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,890 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,058 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
