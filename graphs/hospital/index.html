Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, April 14, 2021, there were 192 people in hospital, 48 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,892 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,468 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,812 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
