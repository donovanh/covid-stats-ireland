Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, March 29, 2021, there were 331 people in hospital, 70 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,501 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,412 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,667 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
