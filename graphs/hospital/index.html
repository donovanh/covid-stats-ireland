Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, April 30, 2021, there were 139 people in hospital, 44 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,232 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,508 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,903 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
