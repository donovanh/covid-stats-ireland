Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, April 24, 2021, there were 162 people in hospital, 46 people in ICU, and 5 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,110 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,495 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,872 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
