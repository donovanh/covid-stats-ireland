Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, December 31, 2021, there were 682 people in hospital, 86 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,662 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,300 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,912 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
