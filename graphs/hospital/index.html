Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, February 5, 2021, there were 1,258 people in hospital, 181 people in ICU, and 35 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,301 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,132 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,621 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
