Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, August 10, 2022, there were 349 people in hospital, 28 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 45,967 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,711 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,743 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
