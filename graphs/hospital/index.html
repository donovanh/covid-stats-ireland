Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, April 23, 2021, there were 166 people in hospital, 48 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,088 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,494 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,867 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
