Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, March 25, 2022, there were 1,466 people in hospital, 55 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 32,555 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,554 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,710 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
