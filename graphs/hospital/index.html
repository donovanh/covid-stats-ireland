Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, March 3, 2021, there were 489 people in hospital, 110 people in ICU, and 25 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,013 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,305 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,357 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
