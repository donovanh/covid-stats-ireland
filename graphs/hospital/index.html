Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, October 27, 2021, there were 503 people in hospital, 101 people in ICU, and 67 deaths were reported.

A total of 18,505 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,980 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,436 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
