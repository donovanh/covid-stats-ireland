Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, February 19, 2021, there were 754 people in hospital, 153 people in ICU, and 28 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,290 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,249 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,109 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
