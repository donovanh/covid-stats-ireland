Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, April 21, 2022, there were 654 people in hospital, 37 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 36,053 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,625 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,993 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
