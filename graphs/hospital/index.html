Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, July 27, 2022, there were 551 people in hospital, 34 people in ICU, and 8 deaths were reported.

A total of 45,087 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,695 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,675 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
