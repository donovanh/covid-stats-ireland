How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, September 23, 2021, there were 286 people in hospital, 63 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,331 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,829 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,209 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
