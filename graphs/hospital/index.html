Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, March 11, 2021, there were 359 people in hospital, 87 people in ICU, and 10 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,176 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,347 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,509 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
