Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, September 8, 2021, there were 335 people in hospital, 59 people in ICU, and 43 deaths were reported.

A total of 15,928 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,757 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,155 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
