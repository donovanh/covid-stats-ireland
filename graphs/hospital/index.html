Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, May 16, 2023, there were 244 people in hospital, 4 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 61,081 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,880 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,935 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
