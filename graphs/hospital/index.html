Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, December 9, 2021, there were 530 people in hospital, 118 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 20,924 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,221 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,788 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
