Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, November 29, 2022, there were 357 people in hospital, 12 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 51,649 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,767 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,197 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
