How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, June 27, 2023, there were 93 people in hospital, 8 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 62,231 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,892 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,063 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
