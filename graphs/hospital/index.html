Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, November 14, 2023, there were 233 people in hospital, 10 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 68,002 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,942 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,366 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
