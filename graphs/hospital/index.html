Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, December 23, 2021, there were 390 people in hospital, 100 people in ICU, and 8 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,608 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,289 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,890 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
