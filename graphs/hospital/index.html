Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 26, 2022, there were 478 people in hospital, 33 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 36,622 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,630 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,028 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
