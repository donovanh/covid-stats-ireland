Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, May 18, 2022, there were 256 people in hospital, 24 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 38,746 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,656 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,244 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
