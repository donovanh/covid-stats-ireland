How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, September 30, 2021, there were 297 people in hospital, 60 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,549 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,849 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,249 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
