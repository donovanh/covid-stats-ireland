Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, February 21, 2022, there were 636 people in hospital, 53 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 28,326 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,492 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,443 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
