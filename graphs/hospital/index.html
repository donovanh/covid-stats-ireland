Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, April 27, 2022, there were 435 people in hospital, 38 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 36,739 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,632 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,054 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
