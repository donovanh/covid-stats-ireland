Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 30, 2021, there were 310 people in hospital, 70 people in ICU, and 14 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,535 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,420 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,681 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
