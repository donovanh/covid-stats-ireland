Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, December 14, 2021, there were 493 people in hospital, 109 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,107 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,245 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,788 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
