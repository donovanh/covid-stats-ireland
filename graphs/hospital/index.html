Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, April 1, 2021, there were 274 people in hospital, 63 people in ICU, and 18 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,593 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,433 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,705 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
