Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 16, 2021, there were 355 people in hospital, 85 people in ICU, and 18 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,150 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,363 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,552 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
