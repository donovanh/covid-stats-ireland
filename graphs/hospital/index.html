Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, May 25, 2022, there were 191 people in hospital, 24 people in ICU, and 9 deaths were reported.

A total of 39,010 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,661 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,304 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
