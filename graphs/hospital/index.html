Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, February 3, 2021, there were 1,366 people in hospital, 202 people in ICU, and 94 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,049 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,093 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,512 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
