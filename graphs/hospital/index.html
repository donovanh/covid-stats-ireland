Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, February 7, 2021, there were 1,203 people in hospital, 179 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,509 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,153 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,686 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
