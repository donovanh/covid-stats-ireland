How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, February 23, 2022, there were 593 people in hospital, 54 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 28,588 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,496 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,460 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
