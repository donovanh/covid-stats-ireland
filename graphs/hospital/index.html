Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, November 27, 2021, there were 536 people in hospital, 121 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 20,245 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,145 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,652 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
