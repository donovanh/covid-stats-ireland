How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, February 15, 2021, there were 916 people in hospital, 158 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,975 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,208 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,948 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
