How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, May 10, 2022, there were 268 people in hospital, 32 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 38,327 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,649 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,167 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
