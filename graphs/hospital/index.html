How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, November 11, 2021, there were 543 people in hospital, 97 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 19,388 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,047 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,566 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
