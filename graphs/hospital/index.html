Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, March 6, 2021, there were 414 people in hospital, 100 people in ICU, and 14 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,123 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,328 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,419 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
