Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, February 18, 2022, there were 591 people in hospital, 58 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 28,048 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,491 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,417 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
