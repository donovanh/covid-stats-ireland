Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, April 28, 2022, there were 412 people in hospital, 36 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 36,882 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,635 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,076 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
