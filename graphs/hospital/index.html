Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 18, 2023, there were 310 people in hospital, 20 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 60,165 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,869 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,824 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
