Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, February 9, 2021, there were 1,104 people in hospital, 178 people in ICU, and 68 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,584 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,174 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,752 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
