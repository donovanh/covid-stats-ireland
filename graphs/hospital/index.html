Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, February 2, 2021, there were 1,418 people in hospital, 207 people in ICU, and 101 deaths were reported.

A total of 10,950 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,080 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,418 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
