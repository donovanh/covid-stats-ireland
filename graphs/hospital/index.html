How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, November 18, 2021, there were 643 people in hospital, 118 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 19,812 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,094 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,609 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
