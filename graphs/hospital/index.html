How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, September 17, 2021, there were 288 people in hospital, 73 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,188 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,812 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,179 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
