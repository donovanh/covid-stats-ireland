Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, April 16, 2021, there were 190 people in hospital, 53 people in ICU, and 11 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,943 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,479 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,831 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
