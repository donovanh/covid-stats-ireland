How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, September 13, 2021, there were 321 people in hospital, 58 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,076 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,776 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,155 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
