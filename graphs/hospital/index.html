Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, July 20, 2022, there were 782 people in hospital, 35 people in ICU, and 7 deaths were reported.

A total of 44,379 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,688 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,620 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
