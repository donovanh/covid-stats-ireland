How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, March 24, 2022, there were 1,425 people in hospital, 53 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 32,311 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,547 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,693 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
