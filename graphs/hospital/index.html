Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, January 17, 2023, there were 370 people in hospital, 27 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 56,027 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,821 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,431 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
