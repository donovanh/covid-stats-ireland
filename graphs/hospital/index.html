Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, February 6, 2021, there were 1,199 people in hospital, 180 people in ICU, and 55 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,440 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,150 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,674 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
