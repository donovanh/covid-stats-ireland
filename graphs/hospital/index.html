Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, December 13, 2022, there were 430 people in hospital, 11 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 52,626 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,776 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,270 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
