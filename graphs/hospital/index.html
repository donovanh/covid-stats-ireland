Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, October 20, 2021, there were 464 people in hospital, 74 people in ICU, and 63 deaths were reported.

A total of 18,017 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,945 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,369 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
