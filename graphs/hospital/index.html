Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, October 17, 2023, there were 240 people in hospital, 15 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 66,991 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,927 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,304 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
