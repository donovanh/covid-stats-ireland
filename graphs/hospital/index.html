Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, December 17, 2021, there were 420 people in hospital, 108 people in ICU, and 7 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,285 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,259 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,835 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
