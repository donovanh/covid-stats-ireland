Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, March 12, 2021, there were 344 people in hospital, 86 people in ICU, and 10 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,111 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,349 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,518 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
