Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, August 24, 2022, there were 285 people in hospital, 17 people in ICU, and 5 deaths were reported.

A total of 46,877 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,717 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,820 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
