Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, January 25, 2022, there were 824 people in hospital, 79 people in ICU, and 7 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,662 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,300 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,087 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
