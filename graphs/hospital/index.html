Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, February 17, 2021, there were 831 people in hospital, 154 people in ICU, and 57 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,127 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,233 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,036 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
