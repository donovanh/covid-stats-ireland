Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, March 19, 2021, there were 336 people in hospital, 87 people in ICU, and 10 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,180 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,390 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,576 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
