Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, February 1, 2021, there were 1,529 people in hospital, 207 people in ICU, and 10 deaths were reported.

A total of 10,949 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,063 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,317 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
