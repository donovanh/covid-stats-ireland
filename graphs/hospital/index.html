Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 23, 2021, there were 357 people in hospital, 76 people in ICU, and 24 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,340 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,411 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,610 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
