Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, February 18, 2021, there were 771 people in hospital, 151 people in ICU, and 47 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,200 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,244 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,082 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
