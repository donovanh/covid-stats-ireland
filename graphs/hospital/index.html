Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, April 28, 2021, there were 153 people in hospital, 45 people in ICU, and 13 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,192 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,502 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,896 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
