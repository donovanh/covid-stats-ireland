Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, March 17, 2021, there were 350 people in hospital, 83 people in ICU, and 17 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,193 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,364 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,566 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
