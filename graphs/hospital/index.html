Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, February 25, 2021, there were 591 people in hospital, 138 people in ICU, and 35 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,724 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,273 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,271 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
