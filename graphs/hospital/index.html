Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, December 13, 2021, there were 518 people in hospital, 108 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 21,087 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,237 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,788 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
