How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, October 1, 2021, there were 308 people in hospital, 59 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,589 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,851 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,249 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
