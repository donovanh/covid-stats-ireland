Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, April 15, 2021, there were 184 people in hospital, 49 people in ICU, and 8 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,919 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,476 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,820 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
