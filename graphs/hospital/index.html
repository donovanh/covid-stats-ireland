How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, September 21, 2021, there were 286 people in hospital, 65 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,245 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,825 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,179 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
