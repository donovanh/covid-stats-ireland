Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, August 1, 2023, there were 270 people in hospital, 9 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 62,943 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,899 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,125 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
