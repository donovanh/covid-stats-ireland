Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, November 10, 2021, there were 551 people in hospital, 83 people in ICU, and 74 deaths were reported.

A total of 19,310 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,041 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,566 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
