How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, November 20, 2021, there were 640 people in hospital, 121 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 19,892 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,105 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,609 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
