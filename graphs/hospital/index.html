Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, February 15, 2022, there were 637 people in hospital, 63 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 27,648 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,478 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,390 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
