Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, October 25, 2022, there were 446 people in hospital, 18 people in ICU, and 5 deaths were reported.

A total of 49,872 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,745 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,043 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
