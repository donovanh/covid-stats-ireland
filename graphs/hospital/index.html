Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, February 27, 2021, there were 550 people in hospital, 134 people in ICU, and 13 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,837 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,292 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,313 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
