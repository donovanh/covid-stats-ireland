How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, October 28, 2021, there were 487 people in hospital, 99 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 18,559 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,990 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,436 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
