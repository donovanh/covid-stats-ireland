Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, February 28, 2022, there were 610 people in hospital, 47 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 28,946 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,504 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,497 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
