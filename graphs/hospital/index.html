Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, November 22, 2022, there were 300 people in hospital, 12 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 51,305 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,760 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,170 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
