Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, April 19, 2022, there were 750 people in hospital, 48 people in ICU, and 5 deaths were reported.

A total of 35,787 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,617 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,964 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
