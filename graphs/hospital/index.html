Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, April 17, 2021, there were 183 people in hospital, 50 people in ICU, and 4 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,960 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,481 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,835 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
