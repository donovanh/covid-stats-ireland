Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, September 26, 2023, there were 323 people in hospital, 19 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 66,035 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,915 requiring ICU attention. A total of 9,249 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
