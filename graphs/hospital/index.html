Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, February 4, 2021, there were 1,308 people in hospital, 187 people in ICU, and 75 deaths were reported.

A total of 11,163 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,116 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,586 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
