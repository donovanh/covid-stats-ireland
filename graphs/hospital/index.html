How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, October 21, 2021, there were 448 people in hospital, 88 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 18,165 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,952 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,369 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
