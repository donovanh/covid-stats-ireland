How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, November 12, 2021, there were 549 people in hospital, 96 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 19,470 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,059 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,566 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
