Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, March 11, 2022, there were 907 people in hospital, 37 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 30,391 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,531 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,611 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
