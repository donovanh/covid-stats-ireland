Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, December 6, 2022, there were 359 people in hospital, 10 people in ICU, and 5 deaths were reported.

A total of 52,139 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,766 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,231 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
