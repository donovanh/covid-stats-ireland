Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, February 7, 2023, there were 178 people in hospital, 22 people in ICU, and 12 deaths were reported.

A total of 56,722 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,838 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,602 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
