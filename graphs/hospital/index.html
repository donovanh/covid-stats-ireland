Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, February 24, 2022, there were 611 people in hospital, 55 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 28,732 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,495 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,471 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
