How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, March 15, 2021, there were 360 people in hospital, 85 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,111 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,358 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,534 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
