Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, November 24, 2021, there were 611 people in hospital, 132 people in ICU, and 43 deaths were reported.

A total of 20,127 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,132 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,652 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
