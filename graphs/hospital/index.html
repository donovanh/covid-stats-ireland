Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, May 4, 2022, there were 347 people in hospital, 36 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 37,718 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,645 requiring ICU attention. A total of 7,103 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
