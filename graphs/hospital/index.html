Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, March 14, 2023, there were 241 people in hospital, 7 people in ICU, and 2 deaths were reported.

A total of 58,309 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,855 requiring ICU attention. A total of 8,719 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
