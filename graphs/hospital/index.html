Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, April 25, 2021, there were 174 people in hospital, 45 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 14,120 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,495 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,873 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
