Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, March 22, 2021, there were 359 people in hospital, 80 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,313 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,402 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,588 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
