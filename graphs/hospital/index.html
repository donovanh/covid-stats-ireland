Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, September 29, 2021, there were 300 people in hospital, 63 people in ICU, and 40 deaths were reported.

A total of 16,503 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,847 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,249 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
