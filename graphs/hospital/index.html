Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Monday, November 29, 2021, there were 579 people in hospital, 115 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 20,377 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,158 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,652 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
