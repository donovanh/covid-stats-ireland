How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, October 16, 2021, there were 406 people in hospital, 71 people in ICU, and 0 deaths were reported.

A total of 17,441 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,930 requiring ICU attention. A total of 5,306 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
