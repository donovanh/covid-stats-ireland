Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Saturday, February 20, 2021, there were 719 people in hospital, 149 people in ICU, and 26 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,326 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,250 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,135 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
