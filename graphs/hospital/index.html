Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Wednesday, March 31, 2021, there were 297 people in hospital, 64 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,566 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,431 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,687 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
