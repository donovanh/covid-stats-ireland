Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Tuesday, February 16, 2021, there were 861 people in hospital, 155 people in ICU, and 33 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,030 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,223 requiring ICU attention. A total of 3,980 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
