Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Friday, February 25, 2022, there were 630 people in hospital, 53 people in ICU, and 1 deaths were reported.

A total of 28,850 people have been admitted to hospital, with 2,497 requiring ICU attention. A total of 6,475 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
