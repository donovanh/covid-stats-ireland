Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Thursday, March 25, 2021, there were 312 people in hospital, 75 people in ICU, and 3 deaths were reported.

A total of 13,395 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,413 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,631 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
