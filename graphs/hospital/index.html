Hospitalised, ICU and deaths

How many people in Ireland are being admitted to hospital with COVID-19 symptoms?

On Sunday, February 28, 2021, there were 554 people in hospital, 134 people in ICU, and 6 deaths were reported.

A total of 12,882 people have been admitted to hospital, with 1,297 requiring ICU attention. A total of 4,319 people have died from COVID-19 in Ireland.
