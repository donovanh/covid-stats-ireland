How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 11,109,287 doses have been given.

A total of 4,060,145 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
