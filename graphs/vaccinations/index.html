How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 1,188,354 doses have been given, enough for about 594,177 people.

A total of 349,710 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
