How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 760,168 doses have been given, enough for about 380,084 people.

A total of 211,223 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
