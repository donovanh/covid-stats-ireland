How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 6,641,944 doses have been given, enough for about 3,320,972 people.

A total of 3,253,742 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
