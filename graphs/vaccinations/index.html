How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 923,878 doses have been given, enough for about 461,939 people.

A total of 268,586 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
