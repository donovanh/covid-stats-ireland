How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 1,076,216 doses have been given, enough for about 538,108 people.

A total of 317,453 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
