How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 606,904 doses have been given, enough for about 303,452 people.

A total of 163,812 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
