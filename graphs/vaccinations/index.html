How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 248,284 doses have been given, enough for about 124,142 people.

A total of 89,380 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
