How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 1,827,610 doses have been given, enough for about 913,805 people.

A total of 499,789 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
