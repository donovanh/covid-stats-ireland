How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 802,502 doses have been given, enough for about 401,251 people.

A total of 224,861 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
