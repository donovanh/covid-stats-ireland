How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 7,137,480 doses have been given, enough for about 3,568,740 people.

A total of 3,618,427 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
