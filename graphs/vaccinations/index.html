How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 654,251 doses have been given, enough for about 327,125.5 people.

A total of 175,526 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
