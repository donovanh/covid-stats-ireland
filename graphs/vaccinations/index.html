How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 1,094,964 doses have been given, enough for about 547,482 people.

A total of 325,243 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
