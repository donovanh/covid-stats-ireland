How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 732,678 doses have been given, enough for about 366,339 people.

A total of 202,694 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
