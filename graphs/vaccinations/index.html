How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 620,580 doses have been given, enough for about 310,290 people.

A total of 165,398 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
