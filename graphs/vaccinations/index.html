How many vaccinations have been given so far?

This graph shows the number of vaccine doses given since Tuesday, December 29, 2020. So far all the vaccinations require two doses, and a total of 639,586 doses have been given, enough for about 319,793 people.

A total of 171,258 people have been confirmed fully vaccinated.
